LaForant is a first-person shooter game. Players will choose between 2 agents in the beginning and each agent has a different story path.

Every player will be given a "classic" gun as a start and a basic ultimate ability.

As the story goes, players will be able to upgrade the character's weapon and ability and play in tournaments with other players to gain more weapons or even get “special side story”.
